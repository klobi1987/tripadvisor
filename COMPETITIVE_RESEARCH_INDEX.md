### Critical Performance Metrics Discovered

**Social Media Impact:**
- Restaurants report **9.9% average revenue increase** from social strategies
- **90%** say social media is very/extremely important
- **40%** of young people prefer Instagram/TikTok over Google Maps for discovery
- Instagram: **2x engagement** for high-quality food photos
- Instagram Reels: **22% higher engagement** vs. other video
- **310M+ photos** tagged #foodporn

**TikTok Performance:**
- **36% of users** ordered from restaurants after watching videos
- **51%** visited after seeing surprising menu item
- Short-form video = **highest ROI** of any social strategy
- **89%** of marketers maintaining/increasing video in 2024

**Review Platforms:**
- **84%** trust reviews as much as personal recommendations
- **62%** will write review if asked
- TripAdvisor algorithm: **Quality × Quantity × Recency** (recalculated DAILY)
- Optimized Google profiles get **2.3x more reviews**
- Photos generate **138% more engagement**, **225% increase** in booking inquiries

**SMS Marketing (MAJOR GAP):**
- **98% open rate** vs. email's 20%
- **24x retention uplift** with SMS campaigns
- Yet adoption is extremely low in fine dining

**Email Marketing:**
- Personalized emails: **6x higher transaction rates**

- Segmentation crucial

**CRM & Retention:**
- **6-7x more expensive** to acquire new customers than retain

**Influencer Marketing:**

## CONTENT BEST PRACTICES SUMMARY

### Food Photography
- **Lighting:** Natural light near windows, soft/indirect
- **Composition:** Rule of thirds, negative space, flat lay for certain dishes
- **Equipment:** Modern smartphones sufficient
- **Impact:** High-quality photos = 2x engagement

### Video Content
- **TikTok:** 9-15 seconds optimal, trending sounds, quick cuts
- **Instagram Reels:** 15-60 seconds, 22% higher engagement
- **YouTube Shorts:** Max 60 seconds
- **Performance:** Highest ROI of any social strategy

### User-Generated Content
- **Trust:** 92% trust individual content over brand
- **Incentives:** Free appetizer/dessert for posts
- **Tools:** Branded hashtag, QR codes, contests
- **Display:** In-restaurant digital displays encourage sharing

### Influencer Collaboration
- **Trend:** Long-term partnerships over one-offs
- **Preference:** 44% of brands prefer micro/nano (10K-50K followers)
- **ROI:** Higher than paid ads
- **Models:** Experience exchange, regular hosting, tracked via codes
