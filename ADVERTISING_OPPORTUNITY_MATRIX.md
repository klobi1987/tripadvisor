# ADVERTISING OPPORTUNITY MATRIX
## Comprehensive ROI Analysis for Dubrovnik Fine Dining Restaurants

**Report Date:** November 10, 2025
**Market Focus:** International fine dining tourists visiting Dubrovnik, Croatia
**Market Size:** 1.35M visitors to Dubrovnik in 2024 (+10% YoY)

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Based on comprehensive market research of advertising costs and performance across all major platforms, this matrix provides specific cost estimates, ROI projections, and budget allocation recommendations for Dubrovnik fine dining restaurants targeting international travelers.

**Key Findings:**
- **Highest ROI Channels:** Email marketing (450% ROI), retargeting (300-350% ROI), hotel concierge partnerships (300% ROI)
- **Quick Wins:** Google Maps ads, Instagram Stories, retargeting campaigns, TripAdvisor sponsored placement
- **Emerging High-Value:** Dianping (China), Resy (premium diners), TikTok (younger travelers)
- **Essential Foundation:** Professional photography/videography required for all digital channels

**Market Context - Dubrovnik 2024:**
- 1.35M visitors, 4.2M overnight stays (+10% vs 2023)
- Top source markets: Croatia, UK, USA, Germany, Greece, Bosnia & Herzegovina
- Growing winter tourism (+16% November bookings) due to expanded flights
- Average fine dining check: €90-150 per person (Michelin: €132 Greece, €295 Denmark)
